Why Chongqing?

The "Cyberpunk" City

In this almost 32,000 Sq. Ft. city, there is an endless amount of things to explore. Chongqing is one of the most developed and technologically advanced cities in the world, and with that there are plenty of amazing things to see. You'll find hundreds of designer stores, but the city hosts plenty of affordable stores as well for all your shopping desires. Don't forget about the delicious food!

Chongqing's Natural Beauty

There's plenty to do in the city, but it'd be a shame if you didn't explore the beauty's of Chongqing's nature. With the countless mountain ranges and caverns to the Yangtze River that flows right by the city, there's an endless amount of natural scenery that leaves everyone in awe.

A City as Old as Time

Chongqing harbors a history that is older than 3,000 years! In Chongqing, you can explore this history by going to the countless museums that showcase hundreds of ancient artifacts, like the Three Gorges Museum, or go to the temples of ancient times like the Luohan Temple!
